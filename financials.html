Financial Transparency

"Honor the Lord with your wealth." — Proverbs 3:9

At The Gospel Foundation, we hold ourselves to the highest standard of financial stewardship. Every dollar entrusted to us is a sacred resource given in faith — and we treat it as such. We believe you deserve complete transparency in how your gifts are used to advance God's Kingdom.

Fund Allocation (Fiscal Year 2025–2026)

By God's grace, we raised $8.6 Million in donations this fiscal year. Here is exactly how every dollar was put to work for the Great Commission:

  • 82% — Direct Gospel Ministry: The vast majority of funds go directly to Bible printing and distribution, missionary salaries, church planting initiatives, and evangelism campaigns in unreached people groups across Africa, Asia, and the Middle East.
  • 10% — Training & Discipleship: Equipping local pastors and evangelists through theological training, discipleship curricula, and leadership development programs to ensure the Gospel takes lasting root in every community.
  • 8% — Administration & Operations: Necessary overhead to maintain our digital infrastructure, process donations securely, ensure legal compliance, and execute outreach campaigns to grow our family of faithful givers.

Independent Auditing & Accountability

The Gospel Foundation undergoes a full, independent financial audit each year conducted by a certified third-party accounting firm. Our books are kept in strict accordance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) and are available for public review upon request.

We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the full extent permitted by law, and annual giving statements are automatically sent to every donor by January 31.

Our Stewardship Pledge

Our leadership has formally covenanted before God and our donors to keep total administrative and fundraising costs below 10% of total revenue — permanently. We do not pay executive bonuses. If operational costs rise, our leadership takes personal salary reductions before any reduction in ministry spending is considered. We exist to serve Jesus and His Kingdom, not to build our own.

Where Your Specific Gift Goes

  • $25 — Provides 5 Bibles in a local language to a village with no access to Scripture.
  • $50 — Covers one week of travel expenses for a missionary serving in a remote region.
  • $100 — Helps fund the launch of a new church plant in an unreached people group.
  • $500 — Sponsors a full month of theological training for a local pastor.
  • $1,000+ — Helps fund an entire evangelistic outreach campaign reaching hundreds of souls.

Non-Cash & Legacy Giving

We also gratefully accept stock donations, cryptocurrency gifts (immediately converted to stable reserves), and planned estate giving. For information on legacy gifts, contact our stewardship team directly at stewardship@gospelfoundation.org.

For detailed financial reports, our Form 990, or corporate matching inquiries, please contact our finance team at finance@gospelfoundation.org. "For we are taking pains to do what is right, not only in the eyes of the Lord but also in the eyes of man." — 2 Corinthians 8:21
