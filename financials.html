Financial Transparency

At Wondrilla, we believe that transparency is the foundation of trust. We are committed to showing our donors exactly where their money goes.

Fund Allocation Snapshot (2025-2026)

  • 85% Direct Relief & Programs: Bulk funding directly into sustainable agriculture, immediate disaster relief, and local educational infrastructure.
  • 10% Logistics & Transport: Shipping, secure material delivery, and volunteer protection.
  • 5% Administrative & Growth: Essential operational overhead, legal compliance, and technological development (including this platform!).

Annual Audits

We undergo strict, independent financial audits annually. Our full Form 990 tax records and detailed audited financials are publicly accessible and submitted appropriately to charity watchdogs and government oversight bodies.

Pledge of Efficiency

We pledge to keep our overhead expenses below 10%, ensuring that your generous contribution heavily impacts those who need it the most rather than internal spending.
