What We Do

Our Mission

We are called by God to take the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the ends of the earth — funding missionaries, planting churches, and transforming lives through the power of His Word.

Bible Distribution

We deliver God's Word in 60+ languages to unreached communities, persecuted believers, and nations where Scripture is forbidden.

Missionary Support

We fund, train, and equip full-time missionaries serving in the hardest and most remote corners of the world for Jesus.

Church Planting

Your giving helps establish new local congregations in unreached people groups, creating lasting centers of discipleship and worship.
