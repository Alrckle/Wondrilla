  1. Create a free account on Make.com or Buffer.
  2. Create a new Scenario/Workflow with a Custom Webhook as the trigger.
  3. Paste the webhook URL above. (It is saved in your browser's local storage).
  4. Connect the webhook to your Instagram, X (Twitter), Facebook, and YouTube accounts in Make.com.
  5. Click "Publish to Channels" to automatically send the copy and generated images to all platforms!
