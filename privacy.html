Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At Wondrilla, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, donate to our causes, or interact with our charity initiatives.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a donation or sign up for our newsletter, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, physical address, and phone number.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and transaction records. (Note: All payment data is processed securely through third-party encrypted processors).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to process donations, issue tax receipts, share impact reports, and communicate updates about our global relief projects. We do not sell, rent, or lease your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

Wondrilla implements industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption and secure firewalls, to protect your personal and financial data. However, please understand that no internet transmission is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal information, request corrections, or ask us to delete your data from our systems. If you wish to do so, please contact us at hello@wondrilla.org.
