Privacy Policy

"You shall not steal, nor deal falsely, nor lie to one another." — Leviticus 19:11

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

The Gospel Foundation ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to honoring the trust you place in us when you share your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our website or make a donation to support our ministry worldwide.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information through the following means to fulfill our ministry mission:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII): When you donate, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us, we collect your name, email address, mailing address, and phone number.
  • Financial Data: We do not store your full credit card numbers or CVV codes on our servers. All payment processing is handled by trusted, PCI-DSS-compliant third-party processors (such as Stripe or PayPal) using encrypted, tokenized transactions.
  • Browsing Data: We may collect basic analytics such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited to improve our website's functionality and reach.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to fulfill our Gospel mission and maintain a relationship with our donors. We use it to:

  • Process and confirm your charitable donations.
  • Issue tax-deductible donation receipts and year-end giving statements.
  • Send ministry newsletters, prayer updates, and impact reports (with your consent).
  • Improve our website and outreach efforts through aggregated analytics.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain session state and remember your preferences. You may disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so may limit some functionality of the donation widget, but will not prevent you from contacting us or accessing ministry content.

4. Information Sharing

The Gospel Foundation will never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Ministry Partners: We may share aggregated, anonymized data with our partner missionaries and church-planting networks for coordinated outreach — never individual personal data.
  • Service Providers: We work with vetted third-party companies for email distribution and secure cloud hosting, all bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when legally required by a valid court order or government mandate, or to prevent fraud.

5. Data Security

We take the protection of your information seriously. We implement enterprise-grade security measures including AES-256 encryption, SSL/TLS data transmission, and routine security audits to safeguard all donor data in our care.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have rights over your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@gospelfoundation.org. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via a notice on our website. Continued use of our platform after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
